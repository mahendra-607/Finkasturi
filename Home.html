About Us

FinKasturi Nivesh Pvt. Ltd.

FinKasturi Nivesh Private Limited is a SEBI Registered Investment Advisor (SEBI Registration No. INA000014128) based in Mumbai. We offer Investment Advisory services to individual and institutional clients. We work closely with investors to maximize yield within the defined investment objective.

With deep experience in the capital market and capabilities to deal with a range of asset classes, we aspire to build an ecosystem of Trust, Integrity, and High-Performance Standards while complying with all regulatory requirements to create a good experience for our clients.

Services We Provide

Our Services

We provide tailor made investment advisory solutions for Individual as well as Institutional Clients. For Individual Clients, we provide advisory services across a range of investments (Mutual Fund, Portfolio Management Service, Provident Fund, NPS etc) and protection (Life Insurance and Health Insurance) products to meet end-to-end financial goals of the client. FinKasturi aspires to be the one stop shop (eg. “Family Financial Advisor”) for client’s needs for investment management. For Corporate & Institutional clients, we provide holistic solutions towards Treasury Management, Risk Management and Asset Liability Management.
